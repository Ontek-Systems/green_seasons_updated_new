Common Questions

Call Tim Directly
What areas do you cover in Oxfordshire?
We are based in Oxfordshire and regularly serve Oxford, Abingdon, Didcot, Wantage, Witney, and the surrounding villages throughout Oxfordshire. If you're unsure whether we cover your postcode, just give us a call and we'll be happy to confirm.
Do you offer one-off visits or only regular maintenance contracts?
We offer both. Whether you need a one-off site clearance to reset an overgrown garden, a seasonal tidy-up, or a fully managed ongoing maintenance programme, we can accommodate your requirements. Our approach is flexible and built around what works best for you.
How quickly can you start?
Start times vary depending on our current schedule and the scope of your project. We aim to provide a quote and proposed start date within a few working days of your initial enquiry. For urgent work, please call us directly on 07545 285408 and we will do our best to accommodate you.
Do you work with commercial clients as well as residential?
Absolutely. We work with a wide range of clients including private homeowners, housing developments, business parks, and local authority spaces. We use professional-grade equipment and are experienced in maintaining grounds to a consistently high standard regardless of size or setting.
Can you advise on planting and garden design, not just maintenance?
Yes, this is one of our core strengths. As second-generation horticulturalists, we offer genuine expert advice on planting schemes, pruning, training, and border design. We go far beyond grass cutting and hedge trimming; we help you make the most of your plants, your space, and your budget.
What weed control services do you offer, and are they safe?
We provide professional herbicide applications for both lawns and hard surfaces, carried out using certified equipment. All treatments are applied responsibly and in accordance with current guidelines to protect your garden, family, and local environment.
Do you provide a free quote?
Yes, all quotes are completely free and carry no obligation whatsoever. Simply fill in the contact form above, call us on 07545 285408, or email green-seasons@hotmail.com and we will arrange a convenient time to assess your garden and provide a tailored quote.
What lawn care treatments do you carry out?
We offer a comprehensive range of lawn care services including full lawn renovation, seasonal fertiliser programmes, targeted weed and moss control, aeration, scarification, and advanced soil conditioning such as wetting agent applications and slow-release fertiliser programmes. Whether your lawn needs a complete reset or ongoing seasonal care, we have it covered.
